Egg Roll in a Bowl

High Protein, Low Calorie

When I was just a small boy, I remember staring out of a window. Just kidding, here's the recipe.

Ingredients

  • Ground Turkey
  • Coleslaw Mix
  • Mushrooms
  • Garlic
  • More Garlic
  • Rice Vinegar
  • Soy Sauce
  • Sauce of Your Liking
  1. Brown the turkey
  2. Sautee the mushrooms
  3. Pan fry the coleslaw
  4. Mix in the vinegar, garlic, and soy sauce
  5. Add in the mushrooms and turkey
  6. Thank me because it's delicious
